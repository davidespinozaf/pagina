My Presentation

Hi students, we are going to introduce ourselves in this section. It's important that you consider some suggestions:

  • Enter the link Padlet: https://padlet.com/britneyleon/personal-information-about-ourselves-cg177lj21vu084xe
  • Each students must make an intervention.
  • To do it, make a new publication y answer the questions. (Check the example)
  • Questions:

1._ What is your name?

2._ How old are you?

3._ Where are you from?

4._ Do you have any favorite hobbies?

5._ Where do you study?

6._ What is your favorite subject in school, and why?

7._ How many people live with you?

8._Are there any places you would love to visit in the future?

9._ What is your favorite type of music?

10._ How do you describe your personality?

  • When making your publication, write your name and surname along with your answers. Remember add a nice photograph of you, add it to your information.
